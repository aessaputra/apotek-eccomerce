# Task 8 Forgot Password privacy evidence

## Privacy implementation assertions
- `error: null` from `requestPasswordReset` shows `AUTH_FORGOT_PASSWORD_GENERIC_SUCCESS_MESSAGE`.
- `isPrivacySafeForgotPasswordError(error)` also shows the same `AUTH_FORGOT_PASSWORD_GENERIC_SUCCESS_MESSAGE`.
- Retryable/rate-limited errors are not treated as generic success; they use actionable localized copy from `getAuthErrorMessage(error)`.
- The screen copy explicitly says success messages do not confirm whether the email is stored in the system.
- The screen/test files do not contain account-existence leak strings.
